Project 1: Digital Account Opening Process Automation

Role: Lead Business Analyst | Duration: 30 Days | Organization: Major Financial Institution

My Approach & Actions

  1. Root Cause Analysis: Used SQL to query 15,000+ rejected applications, identifying that 70% of rejections stemmed from ID validation failures
  2. Process Mapping: Created detailed AS-IS workflow using Visio, mapping all 23 manual steps
  3. Data Visualization: Developed Tableau dashboard showing rejection patterns and bottlenecks
  4. Requirements Translation: Converted business needs into 45+ functional requirements
  5. Solution Design: Collaborated with IT to design Dynamics 365 digital workflow with automated validations
  6. Testing & Training: Developed 25+ test cases, conducted UAT, and trained 200+ frontline staff

Project 2: Automated Case Management System

Role: Business Process Analyst | Duration: 6 Weeks | Organization: Financial Services

Business Challenge

Spike in customer complaints with 60+ day resolution times, no SLA escalation protocols, and 85% of cases being dispense errors requiring manual reconciliation.

My Approach & Actions

  1. Data Analysis: Ingested 10,000+ complaint records using SQL, identifying 85% were dispense errors
  2. Stakeholder Engagement: Conducted workshops with IT, Legal, and Branch Operations teams
  3. Visualization: Created Tableau dashboard showing complaint categories and aging analysis
  4. Process Redesign: Mapped current 15-step complaint process and designed automated 5-step workflow
  5. Solution Implementation: Integrated automated dispense error reconciliation with validation rules
  6. Unified Routing: Designed logic for intelligent complaint categorization and routing
